What is TPTH 2022 ?

The first edition of Tripura Police Technology Hackathon intends to seek technological solution for efficient and effective administration of Tripura Police. We welcome high school and undergraduate students from all technical and non-technical institutes to participate and provide their unique outlook towards the provided problem statements.

Objectives of the Hackathon



Sparking Institute-Level Hackathon fueling your imaginations



Brighten up your skills with the experience of your participation



Enhancing opportunities for citizens to deliver innovative solutions



Crowd-sourcing Intelligence for improving Governance and quality of life



Harnessing creativity and expertise of students, and visionaries

About TPTH 2022

This Hackathon is being organized in association with National Institute of Technology (NIT), Agartala as Knowledge Partner.
