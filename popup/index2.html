Problem:

The fugitive criminal and offenders have lot of social media foot print along with vital demographic data. These data often are speeded in diverse verticals. The objective of this problem statement is to fit in the related data of a wanted individual or back propagation of semantic relation with powerful web scrapping and information revival algorithms to track and establish connection.

Outcome:

A linked map about an individual with cues identified and labelled with source of information.

Major Module:

Simple web interface to accept vital data and available information cues in form of logical input. This will produce a connected information link by crawling vital open source data resources like family register, municipal data, ssn uid data and deep search with web crawling with social media content search.

Platform :

Web based application with Open Source Technology.
