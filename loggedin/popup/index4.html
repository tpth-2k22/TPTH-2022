Problem:

Police Department needs to know the Geospatial Locations where frequent accidents are being occurred across the state. Regular input of lat long, type of accident, case number, casualty etc will be fed into the system. There will be search criteria in ascending/descending order to know the most accident prone area of a particular road etc so that those points may be inspected by a join team of Police and PWD and necessary repair of that portion of the road if required may be taken into consideration. In addition, warning signboard etc may be put for alertness of the drivers.

Outcome:

App based solution or Web based solution is to be developed to know the most accident prone point of a road. In this software accident related data will be fed on a real-time basis directly from place of occurrence. The intelligent reporting and statistical report to be prepared for decision support system for both proactive and preventive manner.
