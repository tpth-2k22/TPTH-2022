Problem:

Identification of vehicle number plate is a major concern of traffic control with law and order maintaining scenario for police department. The task is challenging due to varying lighting condition, speed of the vehicle and in some cases nonstandard numberplates. The task is even more challenging with varying size and shape of a vehicles. In many cases human involvement for continuous surveillance is very difficult therefore an automated number plate identification system is suggested, where a cost effective system can be deployed on highway or crowded road condition.

Outcome:

The identification must occur in varying lighting and climatic condition including rain and fog. The identification must be working with minimum 30 kmph moving vehicle.

Major Module:

Identification of vehicle number plate from video feed input. The application may run on any desktop or single board based portable computer.
